Hiring an Estate Sale Company in Palatka: What You Need to Know

How do estate sale companies in Palatka charge?

Most estate sale companies charge a commission of 25%–40% of total sales. Some may also charge setup fees or minimum guarantees. Always ask for a written contract before signing.

How do I choose the best estate sale company in Palatka?

Look for companies with strong Google ratings and a high number of reviews — this shows a consistent track record. Ask how they price items, how they advertise sales, and whether they handle cleanup afterward.

How far in advance should I book an estate sale company in Palatka?

Top-rated companies in Palatka can book up 4–8 weeks in advance, especially during busy seasons (spring and fall). Contact several companies as early as possible to compare availability and commission rates.
