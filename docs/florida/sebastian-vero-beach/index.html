Hiring an Estate Sale Company in Sebastian / Vero Beach: What You Need to Know

How do estate sale companies in Sebastian / Vero Beach charge?

Most estate sale companies charge a commission of 25%–40% of total sales. Some may also charge setup fees or minimum guarantees. Always ask for a written contract before signing.

How do I choose the best estate sale company in Sebastian / Vero Beach?

Look for companies with strong Google ratings and a high number of reviews — this shows a consistent track record. Ask how they price items, how they advertise sales, and whether they handle cleanup afterward.

How far in advance should I book an estate sale company in Sebastian / Vero Beach?

Top-rated companies in Sebastian / Vero Beach can book up 4–8 weeks in advance, especially during busy seasons (spring and fall). Contact several companies as early as possible to compare availability and commission rates.
